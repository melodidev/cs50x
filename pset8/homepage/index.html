The Greatest Tour

DAY 1

At the morning, we will meet at the rabbit hole and head on to Meadow of Living Flowers for a picnic. To enjoy sunset, we will move to Pool of Tears. You can take an extra tour if you want to sail on. Dinner and accomodation will be on White Rabbit's House.

DAY 2

Have free-time after breakfast at White Rabbit's House. At the afternoon, we will join tea party at Mad Hatter's and then go to Queen of Hearts Castle. You can have a romantic walk at maze, participate a painting activity and book our special suit for night. Dinner and accomodation will be on castle.

DAY 3

After a good night sleep, we will and accompany you to the Rabbit's Hole.

Valentine's Day Special Tour

DAY 1

At the noon, we will meet at the rabbit hole and join tea party at Mad Hatter's. Then, we will take a walk at Tulgey Woods guided by Chesire Cat. At the end of the road, Queen of Hearts Castle will be waiting for us. If you wish you can have a romantic walk at maze. For artist souls, we have a suprise painting activity. Dinner and accomodation will be on Queen of Hearts Castle. We strongly suggest you to book special suit to share a memorable night with your loved one. ;)

DAY 2

After a good night sleep, we will have our breakfast at Queen of Hearts Castle and accompany you to the Rabbit's Hole.
